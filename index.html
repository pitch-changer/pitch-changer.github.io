What is Pitch Changer?

Pitch Changer is a simple but powerful music key changer that lets you change the key of songs, pitch-shift and transpose music without complicated audio software. It's built for real-world use: practicing, teaching, recording or just having fun with your favorite tracks.

You can:

  • Change song key up or down in semitones.
  • Practice in the key that fits your voice or instrument.
  • Prepare backing tracks, karaoke versions and teaching materials.
  • Experiment with arrangements, harmonies and creative pitch shifts.

Pitch Changer comes in two versions: Pitch Changer Online (browser extension, for websites) and Pitch Changer Offline (Mac app, for files).

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Pitch Changer Online and Pitch Changer Offline?

Pitch Changer Online is a browser extension that changes the key of songs directly on websites like YouTube and other audio/video platforms.

Pitch Changer Offline is a standalone Mac app that works offline with your own audio files, and can batch process multiple tracks at once.

Do I need an internet connection to use Pitch Changer?

The Pitch Changer extension runs in your browser and is designed for online content, so you'll typically be using it while connected to the internet.

The Mac app works fully offline with local audio files, so once it's installed you can use it without an active connection.

Which audio formats does Pitch Changer Offline support?

The Mac app is built to work with common audio formats such as MP3, WAV, FLAC and more, so you can quickly process the files you already have without converting them first.

You can also add videos and whole folders — Pitch Changer will extract the audio itself.

Will changing pitch affect audio quality?

Pitch Changer is designed to preserve audio quality while you adjust the key. Our users say that the pitch shift quality is excellent!

As with any digital pitch shifting, very extreme changes may sound less natural, but in most cases it provides clean and musical results.

I can't find instrumental versions of songs, can you help?

Check out our Vocal Remover! It will remove vocals from a song online or offline and it's fully compatible with Pitch Changer.

Is there a free trial? How does pricing work?

Both the extension and the Mac app include a free trial period. No card required!

After the trial, Pitch Changer Online offers weekly, yearly and one-time purchase options, and Pitch Changer Offline offers weekly and yearly subscriptions, as listed above.

All payments are handled securely through Paddle (for the extension) or Apple (for the app).

Just click “Manage Subscription” button inside of the app or the extension and follow the instructions there.

The process is fully automatic — no need to call or write emails.
